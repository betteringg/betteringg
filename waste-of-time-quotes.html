waste of time quotes

December 29, 2022



1. “Time is a created thing. To say 'I don't have time,' is like saying, 'I don't want to.'” – Lao Tzu
2. “The bad news is time flies. The good news is you’re the pilot.” – Michael Altshuler
3. “A wasted life is one that is lived without purpose, without ambition, without joy.” – Rick Warren
4. “Time is the most valuable thing a man can spend.” – Theophrastus
5. “Time is an equal opportunity employer. Each human being has exactly the same number of hours and minutes every day. Rich people can’t buy more hours. Scientists can’t invent new minutes. And you can’t save time to spend it on another day.” – Denis Waitley
6. “Time is free, but it’s priceless. You can’t own it, but you can use it. You can’t keep it, but you can spend it. Once you’ve lost it you can never get it back.” – Harvey MacKay
7. “The key is in not spending time, but in investing it.” – Stephen R. Covey
8. “The great dividing line between success and failure can be expressed in five words: ‘I did not have time.’” – Franklin Field
