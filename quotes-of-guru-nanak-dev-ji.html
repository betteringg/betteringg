quotes of guru nanak dev ji

December 29, 2022



1. "Ek Onkar Satnam Karta Purakh." (Translation: There is only One God: His name is Truth; He is the Creator.)

2. "Na ko hukam hai, jo hukam hai, soi ek hukam." (Translation: No one has command; whatever happens, is by His command.)

3. "Kirat karni: earn your living by honest means, and share with others."

4. "Vichaar jeeo: contemplate and reflect on the divine."

5. "Sarbat da bhala: may all beings benefit."

6. "Man jeetai jag jeet: conquer yourself and you will conquer the world."

7. "Chardi kala: remain in a state of optimism and hope."

8. "Waheguru ji ka khalsa, Waheguru ji ki fateh: The Khalsa belongs to God, and all success is God's."
